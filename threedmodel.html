Jewellery Modelling

To order jewellery modelling services, send us a sketch or a CAD jewellery design you like. our jewellery CAD designers will create jewellery 3D models with utmost precision and professionalism. Furthermore, 3D models can be used to produce photo and/or video  jewellery rendering. This can play a winning role for your commercial purposes.


The most powerful software for 3D modelling are Rhino and Matrix. They are enabling jewellery CAD designers to create different types of 3D jewellery models according to initial jewellery drawing. The mentioned software are giving possibility to show jewellery models in different surfaces and in detail. Our jewellery CAD designers will complete the jewellery modelling process in reduced time keeping the quality of jewellery models. The concept of the model can be produced as a result of discussions between CAD designers and clients.

3D jewellery modelling process can be a real benefit for the clients who can’t find the jewellery model of their ideas in jewellery stores and catalos. You can see the final 3D models before starting the creation of jewellery including the symmetry, proportions and gemstones to be used. Rapid jewellery prototyping is an effective way to show the client the jewellery design and the further jewellery modelling. You can achieve this way the customers needs in maximum amount.
